What Journnah Offers

A comprehensive spiritual wellness companion

Quranic Reflections

Select any verse from the Quran and engage in deep, meaningful reflection. Our guided journaling helps you connect with divine guidance on a personal level.

Morning & Evening Reflections

Start your day with divine guidance and end it with contemplation. Time-based reflection prompts help you maintain a consistent spiritual practice.

Names of Allah

Reflect on the beautiful names and attributes of Allah. Each name comes with guidance to help you understand and internalize divine qualities.

Dhikr Collection

Access a curated collection of dhikr (remembrance) with translations and meanings. Strengthen your connection through regular remembrance.

Personal Journaling

Write your thoughts, insights, and reflections in a beautiful, distraction-free environment. Track your mood and organize entries with tags.

Mood Tracking

Track your emotional state alongside your reflections. Understand patterns in your spiritual journey and personal growth over time.

Review & Insights

Review your past reflections, see patterns, and gain insights into your spiritual growth. Revisit meaningful moments from your journey.

Surprise Me

Let the app suggest verses for reflection. Discover new insights and verses you might not have encountered otherwise.

A Calm Space for Spiritual Growth

Journnah is designed with a minimalist, Stoic-inspired aesthetic that creates a peaceful environment for reflection. We believe that spiritual growth comes from consistent, mindful practice—and our app is built to support that journey.

Whether you're starting your day with morning reflection, ending it with evening contemplation, or taking a moment anytime to connect with divine guidance, Journnah provides the tools and structure you need.

✓ Offline support for uninterrupted reflection
✓ Beautiful Arabic text with translations
✓ Privacy-first: all data stored locally
✓ Dark mode for comfortable reading

Thoughtfully Designed

Every element of Journnah is crafted to minimize distraction and maximize focus. Our warm, earthy color palette and generous spacing create a calming atmosphere that encourages deep reflection. The interface is clean, intuitive, and respectful of your time and attention.
