What Journnah offers

A comprehensive spiritual wellness companion.

i.
Qur'anic Reflections
Select any verse from the Qur'an and engage in deep, meaningful reflection. Guided journaling helps you connect with divine guidance on a personal level.
ii.
Morning & Evening Reflections
Start your day with divine guidance and end it with contemplation. Time-based prompts help you maintain a consistent spiritual practice.
iii.
Names of Allah
Reflect on the beautiful names and attributes of Allah. Each name comes with guidance to help you understand and internalise divine qualities.
iv.
Personal Journaling
Write your thoughts, insights, and reflections in a beautiful, distraction-free environment. Track your mood and organise entries with tags.
v.
Mood Tracking
Track your emotional state alongside your reflections. Understand patterns in your spiritual journey and personal growth over time.
vi.
Review & Insights
Review your past reflections, see patterns, and gain insights into your spiritual growth. Revisit meaningful moments from your journey.
vii.
Surprise Me
Let the app suggest verses for reflection. Discover new insights and verses you might not have encountered otherwise.
About

A calm space for spiritual growth.

Journnah is designed with a minimalist, contemplative aesthetic that creates a peaceful environment for reflection. We believe spiritual growth comes from consistent, mindful practice — and our app is built to support that journey.

Whether you're starting your day with morning reflection, ending it with evening contemplation, or taking a moment anytime to connect with divine guidance, Journnah provides the tools and structure you need.

By design
  • Offline support for uninterrupted reflection.
  • Beautiful Arabic text with translations.
  • Privacy-first — all data stored locally.
  • Dark mode for comfortable reading.
Thoughtfully designed
Every element of Journnah is crafted to minimise distraction and maximise focus — a warm palette and generous spacing create a calming atmosphere, respectful of your time and attention.
