Reflect. Journal. Grow.

A minimalist Islamic journaling app designed to deepen your connection with the Qurʾān through guided reflection and spiritual wellness.

— What Journnah offers —

A comprehensive spiritual wellness companion.

  1. i. Quranic Reflections — Select any verse from the Qurʾān and engage in deep, meaningful reflection. Guided journaling helps you connect with divine guidance on a personal level.
  2. ii. Morning & Evening Reflections — Start your day with divine guidance and end it with contemplation. Time-based reflection prompts help you maintain a consistent spiritual practice.
  3. iii. Names of Allah — Reflect on the beautiful names and attributes of Allah. Each name comes with guidance to help you understand and internalise divine qualities.
  4. iv. Personal Journaling — Write your thoughts, insights, and reflections in a beautiful, distraction-free environment. Track your mood and organise entries with tags.
  5. v. Mood Tracking — Track your emotional state alongside your reflections. Understand patterns in your spiritual journey and personal growth over time.
  6. vi. Review & Insights — Review your past reflections, see patterns, and gain insights into your spiritual growth. Revisit meaningful moments from your journey.
  7. vii. Surprise Me — Let the app suggest verses for reflection. Discover new insights and verses you might not have encountered otherwise.
— About —

A calm space for spiritual growth.

Journnah is designed with a minimalist, contemplative aesthetic that creates a peaceful environment for reflection. We believe that spiritual growth comes from consistent, mindful practice — and our app is built to support that journey.

Whether you're starting your day with morning reflection, ending it with evening contemplation, or taking a moment anytime to connect with divine guidance, Journnah provides the tools and structure you need.

  • Offline support for uninterrupted reflection.
  • Beautiful Arabic text with translations.
  • Privacy-first — all data stored locally.
  • Dark mode for comfortable reading.
— Thoughtfully designed —
Every element of Journnah is crafted to minimise distraction and maximise focus. A warm, earthy palette and generous spacing create a calming atmosphere that encourages deep reflection — clean, intuitive, and respectful of your time and attention.
